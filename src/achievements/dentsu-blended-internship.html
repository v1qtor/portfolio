Blended Internship Program — Champions

This certificate of achievement from Dentsu recognizes my team winning the Blended Internship Program, an international exchange program hosted at Breda University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands, themed Reimagining Third Spaces.

Context

The program centered on data-driven development: we analyzed real data to reimagine customer-centered solutions for third spaces — places beyond home and work where people gather. Working in international teams, we generated ideas and built a prototype showing how a third space could be used to solve a real problem.

What It Represents

Our group finished as champions of the program. For me, this achievement combines the things I care about most: using data to drive decisions, creative problem-solving, and delivering as a team under an international, fast-paced format.
