Blended Internship Program — Champions

This certificate of achievement from Dentsu recognizes my team winning the Blended Internship Program, an international exchange hosted at Breda University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands, themed Reimagining Third Spaces.

Context

The challenge was to use data-driven development to rethink third spaces — the places beyond home and work or school where community actually happens. Digging into the data, our team landed on a real tension: children are getting smartphones at ever younger ages, and screen time is steadily displacing the face-to-face play and interaction those spaces exist for.

Rather than fighting the phone, we flipped it into the solution. We designed QuestSpace, a mobile app concept that uses the device already in kids' hands to pull them outside — turning parks, squares, and other third spaces into playable quests that reward real-world exploration and getting together in person. We took it from data analysis to problem framing, through ideation, and into a working prototype.

What It Represents

Our group finished as champions of the program. For me, this achievement captures the full arc I want my work to follow: start from evidence, frame a problem that genuinely matters, and turn it into a customer-centered product — delivered with an international team under a fast-paced format.
