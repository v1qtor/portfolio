// Biography

I'm an Applied Computer Science student at Thomas More, focused on AI, machine learning, data, and full-stack development. I like clear thinking, practical execution, and building things that actually work in real life.

Before starting my CS degree, I spent about two years at Renaissance Downtown Dubai. I worked across F&B, guest services, and front office, including high-pressure periods where things changed fast. That experience taught me how to stay professional, communicate clearly, and operate within brand standards while still taking ownership when it mattered.

Alongside my academic work, I'm an outdoor sportsman and I enjoy organized sports, especially football because it builds teamwork, discipline, and camaraderie. I also enjoy intellectually stimulating conversations, and I genuinely see myself as a lifelong learner.
